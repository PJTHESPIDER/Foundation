Pollution

Pollution is a major issue that affects the planet. It is caused by the release of pollutants into the atmosphere, which can cause a loss of biodiversity, and a decrease in the quality of life for many people.

Water Scarcity

Water scarcity is another major issue that affects the planet. It is caused by the increasing demand for water, which is being met by the decreasing supply. This has led to a loss of biodiversity, and a decrease in the quality of life for many people.

Deforestation & Soil Degradation

Deforestation & Soil Degradation are two other major issues that affects the planet. It is caused by the cutting down of trees, which can cause a loss of biodiversity, and a decrease in the quality of life for many people.

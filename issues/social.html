Social

There are a number of social issues including Human Rights, Discrimination, and Education that can create divisions in society that can be detrimental to the overall well-being of the population.

Human Rights

Human rights are the rights that all people are entitled to, regardless of their race, gender, nationality, or any other status.

Discrimination

Discrimination is the unfair treatment of people based on their race, gender, nationality, or any other status.

Education

Education is the process of acquiring knowledge and skills.

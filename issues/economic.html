Inflation

Inflation is the rate at which the general level of prices for goods and services is rising and the purchasing power of currency is falling.

Food Insecurity

Food insecurity is a situation where people do not have consistent access to enough food for an active, healthy life.

Economic Inequality

Economic inequality is the uneven distribution of wealth and income within a society.
